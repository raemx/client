Nzube Ufodike

Nzube Ufodike

Entrepreneur with over 20 years experience

Nzube Ufodike is an advisor to early and growth stage businesses, in the UK and emerging markets.

Experience

He was most recently the investment manager of the UK division of a global corporate accelerator, with a UK portfolio of over 250 investee companies and aggregate UK valuation of 1.5B USD. In that role, he was responsible for facilitating millions of pounds in third party funding into Wayra UK portfolio companies such as minicabit and RapTech.

Before that, he held various front and back office investment banking and wealth management roles at PIMCO, UBS, Credit Suisse and Henderson Global Investors. Prior to this, he founded Amoo Venture Capital Advisory where he had the privilege to engage dozens of disruptive clients including Magway, Pavegen, Intern Avenue (acquired by Bright Network), Fits.Me (acquired by Rakuten), and CloudByte. He is an advisor to early and growth stage businesses, in the UK and emerging markets.

More

Involvements

He is passionate about building sustainable and inclusive communities and does this through volunteering; he currently sits on the board of trustees for The Africa Centre, is the Vice-Chair for the British Computing Society Central London Branch, Chartered Institute for IT, and co-leads a wealth creation collective for BlackOut UK.

He is also a member of the board of the Institute of Directors City, and chairs its Young Executives Group.
